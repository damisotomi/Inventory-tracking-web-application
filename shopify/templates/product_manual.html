Product Manual

Adding Products

You can add new product by clicking on the "Add New product" action link above or you can Navigate to 'All products"
link on the left and add new product from there.

Note: When adding a new product, you can only store details of the name, description and group it belongs to.

To add quantities of this product and assign to various warehouses, you have to "Add new product instance/ transaction"

Editing Products

To edit a product, Navigate to "All Products" link, click on the product name, click on edit and update the product

Deleting Products

To Delete a product, Navigate to "All Products" link, click on the product name, click on delete and confirm

Note: You will not be allowed to delete a product that transactions have been recorded for. To do that, you have to delete
the transactions first.
