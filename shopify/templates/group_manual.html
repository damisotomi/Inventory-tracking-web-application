Group Manual

Adding Product Groups

You can add a new product group by clicking on the "Add New Group" action link above or
you can Navigate to 'All Product Groups" link on the left and add new group from there.

Editing Product Group

To edit a product group, Navigate to "All Product Groups" link on the left, click on the product group name
click on edit and update the product group.

Deleting Product Group

To Delete a product group, Navigate to "All Product Groups" link on the left, click on the product group name,
click on delete and confirm

Note: When you delete a group, all products under that group will have their "product group" column set to blank.
