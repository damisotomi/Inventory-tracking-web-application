Product Instances/Transactions Manual

Adding Product Instances/ Transactions

Product instances are also known as transactions. Once you have added details of a product, you can begin
to add product instances or transactions of that product.

Adding product instances or transactions allows you to assign quantities of a product to various warehouses.

You can add new product instances/transactions by clicking on the "Add New product instance/transaction"
action link above or you can Navigate to 'All transactions" link on the left and add new product instance/transaction from there.

Editing Product instances/transaction

To edit a product instance/transaction, Navigate to "All Transactions" link on the left, click on the product instance
name, click on edit and update the product instance/ transaction.

Deleting Product instances/transactions

To Delete a product instance/transaction, Navigate to "All Transactions" link on the left, click on the product instance name,
click on delete and confirm
